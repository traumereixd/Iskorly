Our Solution

Your phone is all you need

Iskorly transforms any Android smartphone into a powerful answer sheet scanner with built-in analytics.

Phone-Based OCR with Teacher Verification

Capture answer sheets with your phone's camera. Our domain-specific parser extracts student answers using Google Vision OCR with an OCR.Space fallback for challenging handwriting.

Teachers review parsed answers in an editable grid before scoring‚Äîmaintaining control and accuracy. Color-coded feedback (green for correct, red for incorrect, yellow for blank) makes verification fast.

Results are stored locally with full privacy protection. Export to CSV for school records or analyze with our built-in masterlist analytics.

How It Works

Five simple steps from capture to insights

1

Camera Capture

Use Camera2 API to capture answer sheet photo with automatic orientation correction

2

OCR Processing

Google Vision primary OCR with OCR.Space fallback for challenging handwriting

3

Domain Parsing

Intelligent parser extracts answers matched to your answer key with Roman numeral support

4

Teacher Verification

Review and edit parsed answers in color-coded grid before scoring

5

Analytics & Export

View MPS by section, item statistics, common errors, heatmap. Export to CSV or JSON

Impact

SDG Alignment

Contributing to global education goals

SDG 4: Quality Education (Primary)

Target 4.c: Increase the supply of qualified teachers and improve teaching effectiveness.

Iskorly directly supports teachers by reducing administrative burden, enabling faster feedback cycles, and providing data-driven insights for instructional improvement. By making assessment technology accessible to under-resourced schools, we help close the digital divide in Philippine education.

SDG 9: Industry, Innovation & Infrastructure (Secondary)

Leveraging existing smartphone infrastructure removes hardware barriers. Our OCR-based approach democratizes educational technology by building on devices teachers already have.

Sustainability

Always Free for Educators

Student project applying to programs; exploring future sustainability options

Current Status

Iskorly is free for teachers and students (no ads, no data selling). We have not raised funds and we are not sponsored yet. For now this is a student project applying to programs; future sustainability options include school/LGU sponsorships, grants, and cloud credits, and on-device OCR to minimize ongoing costs.
